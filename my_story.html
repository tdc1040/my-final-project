My Story

Mental health is something I have struggled heavy with at a young age. There were times were I felt alone and didn't feel a purpose to keep going.
I learned over the years that the best thing I can do is share my story with others. One of the many things that kept me going was thinking of how proud my younger self would be of how I kept going. I love sharing this to others, By doing this, I can build an open community filled with people sharing connections ans spreading positivity.

My goal is to continue what I have created and expand my resoruces I am offering to everyone that needs it.
My team and I are constantly working to unite people together and remind everyone that it is okay to struggle.
